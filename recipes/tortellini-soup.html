Tortellini Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium yellow onion
  • 1/3 Cup all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 2 Cloves minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 Cups chicken broth
  • 14.5 Oz can petite diced tomatoes
  • Italian sausage
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 Cups cheese tortellini
  • 3 Cups spinach
  • 1/2 Cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 Cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, add the onions, flour, basil, garlic, tomato paste, and drizzle with olive oil. Microwave for 5 minutes, stirring every 90 seconds. Add the mixture to the slow cooker
  2. Add broth, tomatoes, chicken, salt and pepper to the slow cooker
  3. Cover and cook on LOW for 4-6 hours or on HIGH for 3-4 hours.
  4. Remove the lid and use a fork to remove the chicken out of the slow cooker. Add in the toertellini and spinach, parmesan cheese, and warmed cream. The cream should be warmed because it may curdle if added cold.
  5. Shred or cut the chicken. Add the chicken back into the slow cooker and cook on HIGH for 10 more minutes or until the tortellini are cooked through.
