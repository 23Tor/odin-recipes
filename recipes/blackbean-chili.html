Black Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic minced
  • 1 Pound ground turkey
  • 3 15 Oz cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 14.5 Oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Bag corn
  • 1 Cup cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 Tsbp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp oregano
  • 1 Tbsp basil
  • 1 Tbsp red cooking wine

Instructions

  1. Cook ground turkey with onion and garlic.
  2. Add into large pot with black beans, crushed tomatoes, corn, and spices.
  3. Cover, simmer on low for 60 minutes.
