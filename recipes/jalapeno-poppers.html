Jalapeno Poppers

Ingredients

  • 4 Jalapenos
  • 8 Slices of bacon
  • 8 Oz cream cheese, softened

Instructions

  1. Slices the jalapenos in half, lengthwise, then remove the seeds by scraping.
  2. Spread the cream cheese evenly on the jalapeno halves.
  3. Wrap each jalapeno in bacon. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapenos on a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes, until bacon is browned and peppers are tender.
