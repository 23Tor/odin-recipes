Garden Salad

Ingredients

    Salad

  • 1 small head iceberg lettuce or other lettuce or leafy greens
  • 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tsp parsley or chives

    • Dressing

  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp cooking/kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  2. Place all Salad ingredients in a big bowl. Pour over Dressing. Toss well.
  3. Transfer to serving bowl and serve immediately!
